# Console Master Blueprint

## Mission
Build an internal console that acts as the command center for:
- product strategy
- intelligence gathering
- evaluation engines
- agent orchestration
- marketing/design operations
- experimentation
- production monitoring
- app lifecycle management

## Core principle
This is not just a dashboard.

It is an operating system for turning intelligence into decisions, and decisions into shipped products and managed systems.

## Core modules

### 1. Mission Control
A top-level view of:
- active initiatives
- active applications
- recent alerts
- key opportunities
- experiments in flight
- system health

### 2. Opportunity Radar
Tracks:
- niche opportunities
- underserved industries
- weak competition zones
- new API/data source openings
- trending behavior shifts
- emerging GTM openings

### 3. Suggestion Engine
Turns signals into:
- what to build next
- what to test next
- what to improve next
- which markets deserve attention
- where risk is rising

### 4. Prediction Engine
Generates probability-weighted views of:
- product success likelihood
- GTM effectiveness
- operational risks
- launch readiness
- market timing
- application health trajectory

### 5. Evaluation Workbench
Used for:
- vendor research
- product comparison
- decision support
- evidence pack creation
- procurement/evaluation workflows
- internal build-vs-buy decisions

### 6. Agent Console
Controls:
- research agents
- monitoring agents
- summarization agents
- scoring agents
- workflow agents
- optimization/self-improvement loops

### 7. App Operations Layer
Tracks internal and shipped applications:
- deployment status
- uptime and incidents
- usage signals
- experiment status
- release history
- dependencies

### 8. Marketing + Design OS
Centralizes:
- positioning
- launch systems
- creative workflows
- asset generation
- brand experiments
- campaign measurement

## Long-term outcome
A world-class internal intelligence system that makes the team faster, sharper, and harder to outcompete.
